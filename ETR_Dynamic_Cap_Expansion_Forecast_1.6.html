Market & Financial Performance

TechiGeneration technology type. Each row shows financial and performance metrics for that technology in the simulation.
New Build(MW)iNew capacity built this year above starting capacity. Calculated as current slider value minus initial ETR configuration capacity.
Ann Cap(MW)iAnnual capacity build limit. Maximum MW that can be added in a single optimization year. Based on Ann Cap Limit % setting.
Capex($/kW)iCapital expenditure per kW of installed capacity. Editable. Used in IRR calculation along with asset life.
Opex($/MW-Mo)iFixed operating expense per MW per month. Editable. Includes O&M, insurance, and other fixed costs.
Life(yrs)iExpected economic life in years. Editable. Used to amortize capex in IRR and levelized cost calculations.
CF(%)iCapacity Factor. Actual generation divided by theoretical maximum (capacity × 8760 hours). Output from simulation.
ELCC(%)iEffective Load Carrying Capability. Capacity credit during top N peak load hours (configurable via ELCC Peak Hrs in Market Policy). Determines capacity revenue.
Energy($/MWh)iCaptured energy price. Average price received per MWh generated, reflecting when the technology dispatches.
Carbon($/MWh)iCarbon cost per MWh generated. Based on fuel consumption, emissions, and carbon price. Zero for non-emitting technologies.
Cap Rev($/MW-d)iCapacity revenue per MW-day. Equals market capacity price × ELCC. Major revenue stream for firm capacity.
REC Rev($/MWh)iREC revenue per MWh for renewable technologies. Based on REC price and generation volume. Zero for non-renewables.
AS Rev($/MW-Mo)iAncillary Services revenue adder per MW-month. Additional revenue stream from frequency regulation, reserves, etc. Editable.
Hurdle(%)iTarget IRR (hurdle rate). Editable. The optimizer builds capacity until actual IRR approaches this target.
IRR(%)iInternal Rate of Return. Calculated from energy, capacity, and REC revenues minus opex, over asset life with capex investment.
